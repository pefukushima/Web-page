Chainsmokers

Joking to make fun of the DJ fame, Chainsmokers released Selfie making instagram bloggers look like fools, but they really didn't expect the success of that hit. A few years later with songs more serious and trying to look like a real duo of djs, they released Roses with Rozes' voice singing and pretty good melody that sticks in your head, even Justin Bieber posted a video singing and commenting that he loved the song which made the djs reach a new level. Later, they decided to try a band album and released Memories Do not Open with a lot of collabs and female voices
